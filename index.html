What Is UNAI?

UNAI Campus by UNAI Multimedia 2022

UNAI is a Higher Education Institution managed by the seventh-day Adventist Church of the western Union of Indonesia. The history UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In the past this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties :

1.Faculty of Philosopy
2.Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3.Faculty of Economics
4.Faculty of Nursing
5.Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6.Faculty of Information Technology
